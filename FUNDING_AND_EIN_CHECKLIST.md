# EIN, Entity & Funding Checklist — QuoteMyAnything, LLC

> Not legal/tax advice — these are the factual steps. Confirm specifics with a
> CPA/attorney. Order matters: do these top-to-bottom.

## A. Get the EIN (free, ~15 min)
- [ ] Go to **IRS.gov → "Apply for an Employer ID Number (EIN)"** (online EIN Assistant)
- [ ] It is **FREE** — never pay a third-party site that charges for it
- [ ] Hours: Mon–Fri 7am–10pm ET; you get the EIN **instantly** (CP-575 PDF)
- [ ] Have ready: responsible party SSN, **Delaware legal LLC name**, formation
      state = Delaware, business address, entity type = LLC, # of members
- [ ] If online rejects you → file **Form SS-4** by fax (faster) or mail
- [ ] Save the CP-575 PDF somewhere safe (banks/lenders will ask for it)

## B. Fix entity good-standing (do BEFORE banking/loans)
- [ ] **Delaware:** LLC franchise tax is a **flat $300/yr**. Dormant 3 yrs likely
      means back tax + **$200 penalty + 1.5%/mo interest**. Check status at the
      **Delaware Division of Corporations** and pay to reach "Good Standing."
- [ ] **Texas:** register the DE LLC as a **foreign LLC** (SOS **Form 304**) and
      file an **Assumed Name Certificate (DBA)** for "QuoteMyAnything."
- [ ] Confirm a **registered agent** in both Delaware and Texas (active).
- [ ] Texas franchise tax / Public Information Report — confirm current.

## C. Business address (protect privacy)
- [ ] Use a **virtual business address with a real street** (iPostal1, Anytime
      Mailbox, or Stable) — ~$10–20/mo, accepted by banks and the IRS.
- [ ] Do NOT publish your home address. Public site shows "Austin, TX" only.

## D. Banking + business credit (turn the aged LLC into leverage)
1. [ ] Open a **business bank account** (needs EIN + formation docs + good standing)
2. [ ] Get a free **D-U-N-S number** from Dun & Bradstreet → starts credit file
3. [ ] Open **net-30 vendor accounts** (e.g., Uline, Grainger, Quill) → pay early
4. [ ] After a few tradelines → **business credit card**
5. [ ] Your **3 years time-in-business** helps unlock LOCs / loans later

## E. Funding reality check
- ⚠️ Avoid "we'll fund your aged/shelf LLC for $50k guaranteed" pitches — most are
  scams. Real lenders verify **revenue + bank statements**.
- ✅ The unlock is **revenue**. That's why the plan is: leads → contractor revenue
  → bank deposits → real loan/LOC applications. Build the top line first.
