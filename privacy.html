Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request, we collect: name, email, phone (optional), ZIP code, and service details. We use this only to match you with local service professionals.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is shared only with the licensed professionals matched to your request. We do not sell your data to third parties, mail lists, or data brokers.

3. SMS/Email Communications

By submitting a form, you consent to receive email quotes from matched professionals. Reply STOP to unsubscribe from SMS anytime. We honor opt-outs immediately.

4. TCPA Compliance

We comply with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA). All forms include a clear consent line. We do not make unsolicited calls without explicit consent.

5. Data Security

Your data is transmitted securely and stored with protection. We do not store payment info (it goes to secure payment processors). We limit access to staff who need it.

6. Analytics

We use local storage (localStorage) for basic analytics. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or third-party trackers. Privacy is built in.

7. Children

Our service is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Email: privacy@quotemyanything.com
