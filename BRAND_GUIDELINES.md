# QuoteMyAnything — Brand Guidelines

Consistency builds trust and makes the brand acquisition-ready. Every page,
post, ad, and asset follows this.

## 1. Name & Legal
- **Brand name:** QuoteMyAnything (one word, camelCase "QMA" short form)
- **Legal entity:** QuoteMyAnything, LLC (Delaware LLC, Texas DBA/foreign reg.)
- **Domain:** quotemyanything.com
- **Slogan / tagline:** **"Free Quotes On Anything!"** (™ — protect it)
- **One-liner:** Get 3+ competing quotes from local pros in 60 seconds. Always free.

## 2. Voice & Tone
- **Helpful first, salesy never.** We save people money and time.
- Plain, confident, fast. Short sentences. No jargon.
- Trust signals everywhere: "Always free for homeowners," "No spam," "60 seconds."
- Value-first in every post: answer the question, THEN mention QMA if relevant.
- Never desperate, never spammy. We're the smart shortcut, not a pitch.

## 3. Colors (locked — already in the site CSS)
| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Background (dark) | `#0f172a` | Page background, primary surface |
| Surface | `#1e293b` / `zinc-900` | Cards, forms |
| Primary accent | `#10b981` (emerald-500) | Logo mark, primary CTAs, success |
| Accent dark | `#059669` (emerald-600) | Button hover |
| CTA gold (service pages) | `#f59e0b` (amber-500) | Service-page submit buttons |
| Text primary | `#f1f5f9` | Body text on dark |
| Text muted | `#94a3b8` / `#64748b` | Secondary text |
| Borders | `#334155` / `zinc-800` | Dividers, input borders |

> Dark theme is the brand. Emerald = trust/free. Gold = action on service pages.

## 4. Logo
- Files in `assets/logos/`: `qma_logo_wordmark.jpg`, `qma_logo_icon.jpg`, `qma_logo_minimal.jpg`
- Mark short form: a rounded emerald square with white **"Q"**.
- Clear space: keep at least the height of the "Q" mark around the logo.
- On dark backgrounds use the standard logo; never stretch or recolor.

## 5. Typography
- System font stack: `-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif`.
- Headlines: bold/extra-bold (700–800). Body: regular. Keep it clean and native.

## 6. Brand Video (the one we feature)
- **Primary brand asset:** the Gemini commercial — `assets/gemini/gemini_qma_commercial.mp4`
- Secondary: `assets/gemini/qma_new_video_2.mp4`
- Featured prominently on the homepage. This is THE brand video. Lead with it.
- (Ads, jingles in `assets/` are secondary/optional — not the primary brand.)

## 7. Messaging Pillars
1. **Free** — always free for homeowners, no credit card, no signup.
2. **Fast** — 60-second form, same-day quotes.
3. **Competition saves you** — 3 local pros compete; you pick the best price.
4. **No spam, no pressure** — privacy-first, your data only goes to matched pros.

## 8. Compliance (non-negotiable, part of the brand promise)
- TCPA consent line on every lead form (already present).
- "Reply STOP to unsubscribe." Honor opt-outs immediately.
- Privacy / Terms / Disclaimer linked in every footer.
- No unverified contact info published (no phone until verified; use virtual address).

## 9. City Expansion Branding
When duplicating for a new city, keep brand identical; only swap:
- City name in titles/H1/schema (e.g., "Free Roofing Quotes Houston TX")
- Local subreddits/Discords and Google Business Profile
- Everything else stays consistent. One brand, many cities.
