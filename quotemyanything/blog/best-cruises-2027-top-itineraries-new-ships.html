Best Cruises for 2027: Top Itineraries and New Ships to Book Now

Updated June 2026 · QuoteMyAnything Cruise Desk

Planning your 2027 cruise vacation? You're in luck — the cruise industry is rolling out incredible new ships and refreshed itineraries that promise unforgettable experiences. With bookings already open for many 2027 sailings, now is the perfect time to secure the best staterooms, prices, and dates before they fill up.

Why Book a 2027 Cruise Now?

Early booking for 2027 offers:

  • Best cabin selection (especially suites, family connecting rooms, and high-demand categories)
  • Lowest prices — fares often increase as sail dates approach
  • More flexible payment plans and deposit options
  • Access to new ships on their inaugural or early seasons

Major lines like Royal Caribbean, Carnival, Norwegian, and MSC have already released significant portions of their 2027 schedules.

Top New Ships Launching or Sailing in 2027

1. Hero of the Seas (Royal Caribbean) — August 2027

The fourth Icon-class ship, Hero of the Seas will be one of the largest and most innovative vessels at sea. Expect the signature waterpark, surf simulator, and incredible entertainment venues. Perfect for families and adventure seekers. Early itineraries likely include Caribbean and possibly Europe or Canada/New England.

2. Carnival Festivale (Carnival Cruise Line) — May 2027

This Excel-class ship debuts with homeport in Port Canaveral. It will feature Celebration Key, Carnival's new exclusive destination in Grand Bahama. Expect 6- and 8-night Eastern and Southern Caribbean itineraries packed with fun, food, and entertainment. Great value for families and groups.

3. Norwegian Aura (Norwegian Cruise Line) — May 2027

The third Prima Plus-class ship brings enhanced dining, larger staterooms, and the signature "Free at Sea" perks. Ideal for foodies and those who love flexible, upscale casual cruising.

4. Other Notable 2027 Debuts

  • Explora IV (Explora Journeys) — Ultra-luxury, all-veranda suites
  • MSC World Atlantic — World-class ship with innovative design and diverse dining

Best 2027 Itineraries by Destination

Caribbean (Most Popular)

  • 6-8 night sailings from Port Canaveral, Miami, Galveston on new ships
  • Highlights: Private islands (Celebration Key, Perfect Day at CocoCay, Great Stirrup Cay), Cozumel, Jamaica, St. Thomas, Aruba

Alaska

  • 7-night Inside Passage or Glacier Bay itineraries on Princess, Royal Caribbean, and Holland America
  • Peak season: May–September for best wildlife viewing and glacier calving

Europe & Mediterranean

  • 7-12 night sailings visiting Italy, Greece, Spain, France
  • Northern Europe and Iceland growing in popularity for 2027

Canada & New England

  • Fall foliage cruises in September–October — stunning colors and charming ports

Pro Tips for Booking the Best 2027 Cruise

  1. Book by end of 2026 for the deepest discounts and best availability.
  2. Consider shoulder seasons (April, May, September, October) for lower crowds and better prices.
  3. Look for bundles — many lines offer free drinks, Wi-Fi, or excursions with early booking.
  4. Check repositioning cruises for longer, cheaper one-way voyages.
  5. Use price drop protection or reprice if fares drop after booking.

Final Thoughts

2027 is shaping up to be one of the best years yet for cruising, with more capacity, new destinations like Celebration Key, and cutting-edge ships. Whether you're a first-timer, family, couple, or solo traveler, there's a perfect sailing waiting for you.
